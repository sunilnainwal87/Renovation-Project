Living Room

Create a warm, stylish, and functional gathering space.

🪵
High Impact

Feature Wall with Natural Stone

Install a stunning floor-to-ceiling natural stone or textured plaster feature wall behind the sofa or TV unit. Pair with warm LED strip lighting for a luxury hotel feel.

Smart Home

Layered Smart Lighting System

Install recessed ceiling lights, dimmable floor lamps, and smart RGB LED strips controlled by voice (Alexa / Google Home). Set mood scenes for movie nights, entertaining, or reading.

🪞
Space Illusion

Built-in Floor-to-Ceiling Shelving

Bespoke joinery units that maximise vertical space for books, art, and decor. Paint the back panel in a deep accent colour to add depth and character.

🪟
Warmth & Comfort

Underfloor Heating + Hardwood Floors

Replace carpets with wide-plank engineered oak flooring over electric underfloor heating. Warm underfoot and timeless in look — a significant home value booster.

🎨
Refresh

Bold Colour Palette Refresh

Repaint in trending earthy tones: terracotta, sage green, or deep navy. Coordinate with new soft furnishings — linen curtains, bouclé cushions, and a statement area rug.

Technology

Concealed Home Cinema Setup

💡 Budget Tips

  • Prioritise structural and plumbing changes first — they're hardest to change later.
  • Phasing work by room can spread costs over 2–3 years without disrupting daily life.
  • Investing in kitchen and bathroom renovations typically returns 60–80% of cost in added property value.
  • Always get 3 contractor quotes and add a 15–20% contingency fund for surprises.
  • Consider energy-efficiency upgrades (insulation, underfloor heating, smart controls) to lower bills long-term.

Suggested Project Timeline

A phased approach to minimise disruption and manage costs.

Phase 1 — Months 1–2: Planning & Design

Hire an architect or interior designer. Obtain planning permission if needed. Finalise drawings, select materials, and secure contractor quotes.

Phase 2 — Months 3–5: Kitchen Renovation

Start with the kitchen as it involves the most trades (plumbing, electrical, joinery). Install bifold doors and skylights during this phase.

Phase 3 — Months 5–7: Bathroom Renovation

Complete wet room, steam shower, and all plumbing upgrades. Tile, fit sanitaryware, and install smart mirror and heated towel rails.

Phase 4 — Months 7–8: Living Room Transformation

Install underfloor heating and hardwood floors. Fit built-in shelving, feature wall, and smart lighting system. Decorate and furnish.

Phase 5 — Months 9–12: Garden & Outdoor Spaces

Build pergola, outdoor kitchen, and plunge pool or hot tub. Landscape, plant four-season scheme, and install outdoor lighting and audio.

Phase 6 — Month 12+: Finishing & Styling

Final snagging, professional cleaning, interior styling, landscaping touch-ups, and professional photography to document the transformation.
